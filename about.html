Our Story

Locally Owned &
Operated in Citrus County

No corporate runaround. Just honest house cleaning from locals who actually show up.

Hi, we're Hunter & Jon.

We started Evergreen Cleaning Services because we saw how hard it was for homeowners in Citrus County to find a cleaning service that was actually reliable — one that showed up when they said they would, did great work, and didn't overcharge.

So we built that service ourselves. We're locals, we know these neighborhoods, and we genuinely care about doing right by the people we work for. When you book with Evergreen, you're not calling a call center — you're calling us.

We serve Citrus County and surrounding areas. Every clean is done with eco-friendly supplies, priced by the size of your home, and backed by our 5-star service guarantee. No shortcuts, no add-ons you didn't ask for, no surprises on the invoice.
