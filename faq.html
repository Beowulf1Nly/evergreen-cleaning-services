Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our cleaning service — pricing, scheduling, supplies, and more.

Pricing

How much does a house cleaning cost? +
Pricing is flat-rate and based on your home's size — roughly $50 per hour of work with a $79 minimum. Standard cleans start at $79, deep cleans at $99, and move-in/out cleans at $119. A 3 bed · 2 bath standard clean is $149. Get your exact price with the instant quote builder on our home page — what you see is what you pay.
What's the difference between a standard clean and a deep clean? +
A standard clean covers regular upkeep: dusting, vacuuming, mopping, kitchen surfaces, and full bathrooms. A deep clean adds the stuff that builds up over time — baseboards, door frames, tile grout, appliance detailing, ceiling fans, vents, and window sills. If it's our first visit or it's been a while since a professional clean, we recommend starting with a deep clean.
What's included in a move in/out clean? +
Everything in the deep clean, plus inside all cabinets, drawers, and closets, inside the oven and refrigerator, and spot-cleaning of walls and doors. It's built for empty homes — perfect for moving day, landlords, and getting deposits back.
Is there a sign-up fee or any hidden costs? +
None. The flat rate for your home size is what you pay. We don't charge sign-up fees, supply fees, or any other hidden costs.

Scheduling & Service

Do I need to be home during the cleaning? +
Nope. As long as we can access your home, you're all set. Many customers leave a key, garage code, or lockbox instructions in their booking notes. We'll let you know when we're done.
Do you bring your own supplies and equipment? +
Yes — we bring everything, including eco-friendly cleaning supplies that are safe for kids and pets. If you'd prefer we use specific products you provide, just let us know in your booking notes.
How long does a cleaning take? +
It depends on the size of your home and type of clean. A standard clean of a 2-3 bedroom home usually takes 2–3 hours. Deep cleans and move-in/out cleans take longer — typically 4–6 hours. We'll give you a time estimate when we confirm your booking.
What about pets? +
We love pets! Just let us know about them in your booking notes so our cleaner knows what to expect. We only use pet-safe, eco-friendly products. If your pet gets stressed by visitors, we recommend keeping them in a comfortable spot while we work.

Billing & Cancellation

When and how do I pay? +
You pay after the clean is done and you're happy with it — not upfront. We'll confirm payment options when we confirm your booking.
Can I cancel or reschedule? +
Yes — no contracts, no cancellation fees, ever. Just call or text us at (352) 462-0627 before your scheduled day and we'll take care of it.
What if I'm not happy with the clean? +
Contact us within 48 hours and we'll come back and re-clean the areas in question for free. If we can't make it right, we'll refund that visit. See our full Refund Policy for details.
